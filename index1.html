History

With a graceful charity of rupees 2 lacs (in 1960 A.D) donated by his highness Shri Vitthalbhai Shivabhai Patel, V.S. Patel College of Arts & Science, Bilimora was started on 15th June 1961. Because of unavailability of infrastructure, 4 classes which included 2 of Science previous and the other 2 of inter Arts were inaugurated by late Shri Thakorbhai Manibhai Desai at Sir J.J. Mehta High School, Bilimora. The trustees of Sir J.J. Mehta High School offered this intermediate provision without any expectation of monetary remuneration.

The proposed college got an affiliation of Gujarat University, Ahmedabad. After that, Shri Naushir Manchersha Bilimoria donated a piece of vast land on Chikhli-Bilimora road, for a new building of V.S. Patel College, after the name of his father late Manchersha Burjorji Mengusi, where presently the whole campus is named as Mengusi Park.

The foundation stone of the new college building was laid down by the Chief Minister of Gujarat State at that time, late Shri Hitendrabhai Desai. The college, which was started with merely four classes, has now bulged up into a big college comprising various sophisticated, well-equipped departments glorified with well-trained subjective teachers and vast, well-furnished classrooms. Beside the main building, the college comprises a chemistry laboratory for postgraduate courses, which was developed by the graceful charity of Shri Kantidev Jethabhai Patel and Shri Kanubhai Zaverbhai Patel (BIPICO industry, Bilimora).

The principal courses like Hindi, Gujarati, and Economics are offered at an undergraduate level for a bachelor's degree, which also includes Psychology and Statistics as subsidiary subjects. At the postgraduate level, Hindi is offered to the students of the Arts faculty.

Similarly, the principal courses like Chemistry, Mathematics, Statistics & Physics (Self Financed) are offered at the graduate level, and Chemistry is offered at the postgraduate level. Along with this, Physics, Botany, and Statistics are offered as subsidiary subjects to the science students at the graduate level.

Till date, many of the staff faculties have published their research and popular articles in various national & international journals. Some of them have also represented the college on television and radio.

With a noble aspect of developing innovative talent among the students of the college, we are publishing a yearly magazine called TRIVENI, where the students who come from different social, cultural & educational backgrounds reflect their ingenuity in the form of research and artistic articles.

The authorities, including teaching and non-teaching staff, are making continuous efforts to imbibe and upsurge a notion for social welfare among the students through various schemes like National Cadet Corps, National Service Scheme, Planning Forum, etc. As and when the need from the various sections of society, especially during natural calamities like floods, earthquakes, epidemics, etc., have arisen, these cells have proved to be efficient.

This institution has hosted many conferences, seminars & sports events of district, university, and state levels.

And last but not least, our college, which has been continuously trailing the path for its all-round betterment, would have been next to impossible without the motherly care graced from our respectable & profusely respectable Principal. They have proved themselves to be the stakeholders in each and every aspect, and for the all-round development of our college.

Management

Structure of Bilimora Vibhag Kelvani Mandal

The management of V.S. Patel College of Arts & Science is governed by the Bilimora Vibhag Kelvani Mandal, which oversees the administration and development of the institution. The Mandal ensures that the college adheres to its mission of providing quality education and fostering innovation.

Principal's Message

Dear students and all other stakeholders: I am greatly honored to be associated with the field of education. Education is universally recognized as a fundamental building block for human development and one of the strongest instruments for reducing poverty.
